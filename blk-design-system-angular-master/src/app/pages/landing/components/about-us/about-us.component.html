About Us

Established in 2007 and headquartered in Miami. Blue Crew Contractors is a leading contracting company, construction manager and consulting group. Blue Crew was founded upon quality, experience, impeccable references and best-of-class staffing and services.


With many years of combined industry experience, Blue Crew team understand the goals of clients and are able to institute creative, proactive and effective solutions to their specific needs.
