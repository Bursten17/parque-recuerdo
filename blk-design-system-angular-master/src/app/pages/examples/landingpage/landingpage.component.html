About Us

Established in 2007 and headquartered in Miami. Blue Crew Contractors is a leading contracting company, construction manager and consulting group. Blue Crew was founded upon quality, experience, impeccable references and best-of-class staffing and services.


With many years of combined industry experience, Blue Crew team understand the goals of clients and are able to institute creative, proactive and effective solutions to their specific needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a construction plan that is unique and specific to the needs of each client. We will dedicate our considerable talents and experience to detail a PLAN that will complete the project safely, on time, within budget and to the desired level of quality, by actively participating in a process-driven, team oriented setting to conceptualize, develop, buy and execute the PLAN.


WOur reputation will always be driven by results and we will generate new business primarily through superior references.

Characteristics

Reliability

Projects are always successfully completed on time and within budget. We provide cost saving option whenever possible.

Expertise

We have extensive experience in renovating buildings with occupants and sensitive items with minimal disruption in specialized attention to health and safety.

Responsiveness

We collaborate closely with all projects stakeholders in the decision making process, are flexible, quickly implement innovative solutions to complex issues and provide regular updates to ensure success.

Community

Jorge W. Liza

Jorge W. Liza, President and Chairman of the Board of Blue Crew Contractors, has been at the forefront of South Florida’s construction for over 13 years. A commitment to building better cities. First arriving in Miami Florida June of 2001. “This city was beautiful, It felt like home so I decided to stay and grow here”. Mr. Liza had several jobs from Technitian to Project Executive in his familie’s construction business. Coming from a family of Builders “ I learned the value of a happy client”. On 2007, the dream started and rapidly became a strong company in the industry. Over the years, Liza and Blue Crew have partnered with great architects and interior designers to make the company stronger and improve the construction environment.
